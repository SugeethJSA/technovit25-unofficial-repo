### Event Details

InnoHack is a Workshop on innovation and IPR followed by a hackathon.

**Objective:**

* To educate students about intellectual property rights (IPR) and their role in innovation.
* To inspire students to generate creative solutions to real-world problems.
* To provide a platform where students can implement their ideas through a hackathon - Group activity.

**Tentative Sessions:**

* Session 1: Introduction to Innovation (FN session)
* Session 2: Understanding intellectual property rights (IPR) (FN session)
* Session 3: Ideation & Creativity (FN session)
* Session 4: Hackathon - Group activity - Creative solutions to real-world problems (FN & AN session)
* Session 5: Presentation (AN session)

Participants learn to implement ideas effectively.
